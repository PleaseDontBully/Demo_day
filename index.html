About Us

Hi, Welcome to Phoenix Hotline Resources. We are a group of highschoolers attempting to make the world a better place. We started off taking a coding intensive at All Star Code, learning about all different types of programming languages. During our last project we were tasked to make a website or app that would impact the world in a positive way. So thats why we created the Pheonix Hotline. Our goal for the Pheonix hotline is to attempt to save as many lives as possible, gathering as many helpful sites as we can and collecting them in an easy to find website.
